# T023 — 🔒 Human review: app icon quality

## Overview

A human eyeballs the generated app icon before it's considered final — script-generated art needs a
taste check the machine can't self-certify.

## Do

(Human, interactively:)

1. Open `Sources/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/AppIcon.png` full-size: near-black ground,
   two faint rings, bright green center dot, soft green sweep wedge — matches the design's 3b panel
   (`design/design_handoff_scout/Scout.dc.html`).
2. Check small-size legibility: view the PNG scaled to ~120 px and ~60 px (Preview zoom or
   `sips -Z`). The design brief requires it to read clearly at 60 pt and 40 pt — the dot and at
   least one ring must remain visible.
3. Install on the simulator or a device (`./build_run.sh`, then press Home in the sim) and look at
   it on the actual home screen next to system apps: mask cropping OK, not too dim.
4. If it fails taste: capture what's wrong as a new idea/backlog task (e.g. thicker rings at small
   sizes) rather than hand-editing the PNG.

## Done when

The owner has looked at all three (full-size, small-size, on-home-screen) and accepts it — then run
`.harness/scripts/mark-done.sh T023`. If rejected, file the follow-up and still mark this review
done once a decision is recorded.
